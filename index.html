Hi ladies and gents, finally I have built something for myself. A portfolio and blog that shows how I love web design, html and css coding. I love to discover new stuffs building and designing web. I look forward to creating more websites to the best of my knowledge.

I am a father of my only 1 cute daughter Anika Sealtiel and husband of my lovely and supportive wife Vangie. You might be wondering why I call my site "Asta" , this means "A"nika "S"ealtiel(My daughter's name) "T"an(my wife's family name) "A"rreola, sounds weird huh? :)

I graduated Information Technology course from Saint Louis University at the City of Baguio. Started as a Visual Basic 6 programmer and later on attached to Photoshop/graphics design, HTML and CSS.

From there, it was history. I had an office job as a web designer/front-end developer for over 6 years but right now I enjoy the freedom of working at home. I try to develop my skills every now and then as much as I can and to share it to aspiring web designers out there.
